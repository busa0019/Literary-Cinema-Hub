# Literary Resources

Helpful tools, platforms, and resources for readers and writers.

## Reading Platforms

### Book Tracking & Discovery
- **Goodreads** - Social reading platform with reviews and recommendations
- **StoryGraph** - Alternative with better analytics and content warnings
- **LibraryThing** - Catalog your personal library
- **BookBub** - Deals and recommendations

### E-book Resources
- **Project Gutenberg** - Free public domain ebooks
- **Standard Ebooks** - High-quality formatted public domain books
- **Open Library** - Digital lending library
- **Libby/OverDrive** - Library e-book borrowing

### Audiobook Platforms
- **Audible** - Amazon's audiobook service
- **Libro.fm** - Supports local bookstores
- **LibriVox** - Free public domain audiobooks
- **Scribd** - Subscription service with ebooks and audiobooks

## Writing Resources

### Writing Tools
- **Scrivener** - Powerful writing software for long projects
- **Ulysses** - Minimalist writing environment
- **Grammarly** - Writing assistant and grammar checker
- **Hemingway Editor** - Readability analysis

### Learning Resources
- **Brandon Sanderson's Writing Lectures** (free on YouTube)
- **Neil Gaiman's MasterClass** on Storytelling
- **Writing Excuses Podcast** - 15-minute writing advice
- **NaNoWriMo** - National Novel Writing Month community
