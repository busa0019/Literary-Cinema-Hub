# Favorite Authors

## Contemporary Masters

### Brandon Sanderson
**Genre**: Fantasy, Science Fiction  
**Notable Works**: Mistborn series, Stormlight Archive  
**Why I Love**: World-building mastery and unique magic systems

<div class="book-card">
  <h3>Author Spotlight: Brandon Sanderson</h3>
  <p><strong>First Book Read:</strong> Mistborn: The Final Empire</p>
  <p><strong>Favorite Work:</strong> The Way of Kings</p>
  <p><strong>Why I Recommend:</strong> Sanderson creates incredibly detailed worlds with magic systems that feel both magical and scientific.</p>
</div>

### Emily St. John Mandel
**Genre**: Literary Fiction, Speculative Fiction  
**Notable Works**: Station Eleven, The Glass Hotel  
**Why I Love**: Beautiful prose and thought-provoking premises

### T.J. Klune
**Genre**: Fantasy, Contemporary  
**Notable Works**: The House in the Cerulean Sea, Under the Whispering Door  
**Why I Love**: Heartwarming stories with deep emotional resonance

## Literary Legends

### Jane Austen
**Genre**: Classics, Romance  
**Notable Works**: Pride and Prejudice, Emma  
**Legacy**: Social commentary through romantic narratives

### Gabriel García Márquez
**Genre**: Magical Realism  
**Notable Works**: One Hundred Years of Solitude, Love in the Time of Cholera  
**Legacy**: Pioneered magical realism in Latin American literature

## Rising Stars

### R.F. Kuang
**Genre**: Fantasy, Historical Fiction  
**Notable Works**: The Poppy War, Babel  
**Emerging Voice**: Blends historical events with fantasy elements
