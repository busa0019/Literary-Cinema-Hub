# Personalized Recommendations

Curated book and movie suggestions based on different tastes and moods.

## Based on Your Tastes

### If you like complex world-building:
- **The Stormlight Archive** by Brandon Sanderson
- **The Three-Body Problem** by Cixin Liu
- **The Name of the Wind** by Patrick Rothfuss

### If you enjoy character-driven stories:
- **A Little Life** by Hanya Yanagihara
- **The Goldfinch** by Donna Tartt
- **Normal People** by Sally Rooney

### If you prefer fast-paced plots:
- **Dark Matter** by Blake Crouch
- **The Martian** by Andy Weir
- **The Da Vinci Code** by Dan Brown

## Mood-Based Recommendations

### For when you need comfort:
- **The House in the Cerulean Sea** by T.J. Klune
- **Anne of Green Gables** by L.M. Montgomery
- **Good Omens** by Neil Gaiman & Terry Pratchett

### For when you want to think:
- **Klara and the Sun** by Kazuo Ishiguro
- **Station Eleven** by Emily St. John Mandel
- **Sapiens** by Yuval Noah Harari

### For when you need adventure:
- **The Hobbit** by J.R.R. Tolkien
- **Mistborn** by Brandon Sanderson
- **Jurassic Park** by Michael Crichton
